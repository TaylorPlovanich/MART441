What I See When I Look at the World

When I look at the world, I see a complex interplay of beauty, challenges, and potential. Our planet is full of vibrant colors, diverse cultures, and innovative ideas. However, it also faces significant issues such as climate change, inequality, and the need for more meaningful connections. I believe games can be a tool to inspire empathy, problem-solving, and awareness, bringing people together to tackle some of these challenges.

Interests and Gaming Memories

One of my fondest memories is playing The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time as a child. It taught me the value of perseverance, exploration, and problem-solving. Today, I still find joy in games that challenge my creativity and critical thinking. I believe games can help us solve real-world problems by simulating scenarios, encouraging collaboration, and inspiring new ways of thinking.

Outside of gaming, I enjoy hiking in the mountains of Montana, experimenting with new art techniques, and exploring the potential of virtual and augmented reality in storytelling.
